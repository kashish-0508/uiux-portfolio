ABOUT ME

Designing with curiosity.
Building with purpose.

I'm a UI/UX Designer & Frontend Developer with a B.Tech in Computer Science from COER University. I enjoy transforming ideas into intuitive digital experiences and bringing them to life through clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My blend of design thinking and development allows me to bridge the gap between user needs and technical implementation.

Alongside development, I collaborated on improving usability, creating reusable UI components, and ensuring a seamless experience across devices. I also design wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity interfaces in Figma, combining creativity with practical implementation to deliver polished digital products.
